Episode I: The Phantom Menace

Plot

Two Jedi Knights escape a hostile blockade to find allies and come across a young boy, Anakin Skywalker. A prophecy suggests Anakin may bring balance to the Force, but the long-dormant Sith resurface to reclaim their old glory.

Ending

Darth Maul makes a (depressingly early) exit from the franchise. Jar-Jar is killed in a freak Podracer accident.

Episode II: Attack of the Clones

Plot

Ten years after their first meeting, Anakin Skywalker shares a forbidden romance with Padme, while Obi-Wan investigates an assassination attempt on the Senator and discovers a secret clone army crafted for the Jedi.

Ending

Politics and complicated stuff. This movie is the nadir of the franchise.

Episode III: Revenge of the Sith

Plot

After three years of fighting in the Clone Wars, Anakin Skywalker falls prey to the Sith Lord's lies and makes an enemy of the Jedi and those he loves, concluding his journey to the Dark Side.

Ending

Anakin cosplays as Darth Vader!

Episode IV: A New Hope

Plot

The secret to ending the domination of the Galactic Empire is carried by two droids, sent on a mission to find the last Jedi. They find a rustic farmboy, Luke Skywalker, who dreams of leaving his monotonous life for adventure.

Ending

Luke Skywalker joins a cocky smuggler, a furry pilot, and a princess and together, they defeat the Galactic Empire. (FOR NOW!)

Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back

Plot

Darth Vader and the Galactic Empire pursue Luke Skywalker and the rest of the Rebel Alliance. After Vader captures Luke's friends, Luke must decide whether to complete his Jedi training or confront Vader to save his comrades.

Ending

Luke... I am your father. Also: AT-ATs and Boba Fett.

Episode VI: Return of the Jedi

Plot

After rescuing Han Solo from the palace of Jabba the Hutt, the Rebels attempt to destroy the Second Death Star, while Luke Skywalker tries to bring his father back to the Light Side of the Force.

Ending

The Death Star explodes [again] and the Ewoks throw a party in their tree village!
